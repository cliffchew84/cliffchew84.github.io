Findings
  • Spreading of million dollar resale public flats in Singapore
  • Not just growth in million dollar flats, but a shift in general pricing across all categories
    • As recent as 2020, 70% of public resale flats were half a million SGD ( 11 million NTD ) and below
    • By 2024, 70% of public resale flats were half a million SGD ( 11 million NTD ) and above
    • By 2025, we are reaching 80%...
  • Among lowest 10th percentile public homes, there is an increasing segment selling right after they are qualified to do so
