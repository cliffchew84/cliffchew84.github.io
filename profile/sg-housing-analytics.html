SG Public Housing Analytics

Beyond my general housing trend dashboard and public housing transaction search dashboard, I am doing some deeper analytics research based on our Singapore housing market. These are longer analyses where I do deeper dives into our Singapore housing market, based on data provided by our Singapore government. These analyses strive to provide more insights into the nature of our Singapore housing market. While some of the posts are initially published on my Medium blog, the benefit of publishing them here is that I can add interactively plots that my users can also play with themselves.

Hopefully, these interactive charts can help my audience better understand the housing data that I am looking at and playing with myself. Ultimately, I hope that these analyses can allow us to have wider discussions about our Singapore housing market in general.
