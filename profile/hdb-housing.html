SG Public Housing Dashboard

Singapore's public housing policies are internationally acclaimed, with many attributing our economic miracle to our public housing policies. However, there is recent discussions on the affordability of our public homes amongst Singaporeans. Since the public home resale data set is publicly available, I decided to try to let the data speak for itself, by creating a dashboard to monitor our public housing market in my own way.

Rather than focus on single metrics like median or mean prices, I wanted to plot out the quarterly public resale price distributions. We can see that the distribution of public home sales have widened quite a bit in the past few years. This shows that not only are we having public homes that are selling for record, beyond million dollar prices, we still have some really affordable public homes in our resale market.

On the other hand, we cannot deny the growing number of million dollar public homes sold in Singapore in recent years. Focusing on this metric definitely feels like our Singapore public housing market is becoming less and less affordable.
