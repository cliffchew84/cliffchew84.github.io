About Me

Cliff Chew

Analytics Consultant & Trainer

As an NUS Economics major, I always felt my route into data analytics was less direct. When I first graduated in 2009, Data Science wasn't much of a term yet in Singapore. As a lost soul swept up by the Lehman Brother's collapse of '08, I jumped through a Master's programme and hopped through several jobs that I had no real interest in. Along the way, I learned about the data science industry, picked up Python programming language on my own, spent three years building a prediction model and applied to many different tech companies for any analytics role possible, all before finally hitting my first career break in 2016.

  • 2016 - Joined Carousell as their first Data Analyst in their digital marketing team.
  • 2019 - Joined Grab as their Senior Data Analyst to deal with fraud on their platform.
  • 2021 - Became a freelancer consultant and trainer to bring my analytics expertise to other smaller startups, small medium enterprises and analytics mid-career switchers.

Since then, I have worked with clients and teams from Hong Kong, Shenzhen, Taiwan, Singapore, Malaysia and even London. I worked on both being a full-stack developer ( still lousy at this ), worked on business and product development, and further honed my skills as an analytics trainer and consultant. I hope my portfolio and writings can help spread the possibilities of tech and analytics to more professionals and organisations.
