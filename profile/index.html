About Me

Cliff Chew

Analytics Consultant & Trainer

As an NUS Economics major, my route into data analytics always felt rather surreal. Data Science wasn't much of a term yet in Singapore when I first graduated in 2009. As a lost soul swept up by the Lehman Brother collapse of 08, I jumped through a Master's programme and hopped through several jobs. Along the way, I learned about the data science industry, self-taught Python programming language, spent three years building a prediction model and applied to countless tech companies for any kind of analytics role. I finally landed my first career break in a local tech startup.

  • 2016 - Joined Carousell as their first Data Analyst, supporting their digital marketing team.
  • 2019 - Joined Grab as their Senior Data Analyst to tackle various form of fraud on their platform.
  • 2021 - Became a freelancer consultant and trainer, with the aim of bringing my analytics expertise to smaller startups and small medium enterprises, while also supporting analytics mid-career switchers on their analytics journey.

Since then, I have worked with client teams from Hong Kong, Shenzhen, Taiwan, Singapore, Malaysia and London. I worked on full-stack development ( still lousy at this ), product development and business development, while continuing to hone my skills as a trainer and consultant. The purpose of my portfolio page and various writings is to spread the possibilities of tech and analytics to more professionals and organisations.
